CandyCrash3 does not collect personal information that can identify individual users. We DO NOT collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or contact details.

We MAY collect non-personal game data:

HOW WE USE INFORMATION

Non-personal information is used to:

DATA SECURITY

We implement appropriate security measures to protect non-personal data from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. All data is encrypted during transmission and stored securely.
